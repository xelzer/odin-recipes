Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a delicous pizza combining our usual mozarella with grated mozarella resulting in a tasteful delight that is truly irresistable

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Wheat
  • Mozarella
  • Buffallo Mozarella
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Peppers

Steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs and milk into dough
  2. Spread your tomato sauce over the dough
  3. Spread your mozarella evenly across the pizza
  4. Place buffallo mozarella and peppers evenly across the pizza
  5. Cook in the oven at 180℃
  6. Enjoy!
