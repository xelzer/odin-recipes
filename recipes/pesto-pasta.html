Pesto Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a savoury pesto pasta with a rich, creamy pesto sauce that will leave you wanting more and more

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto of your choice
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta al dente
  2. Throw pasta into a frying pan with other desired ingredients and pesto for 3 minutes
  3. Serve and enjoy!
